<p>Ever stand in front of a full closet and feel like you have nothing to wear? A <strong>capsule wardrobe</strong> fixes exactly that. It's a small, carefully chosen collection of pieces that all work together — so almost everything you own can be combined into an outfit. Fewer items, more looks, zero decision fatigue. Here's how to build one.</p>

<h2>What is a capsule wardrobe?</h2>
<p>A capsule wardrobe is a curated set of versatile, timeless pieces — usually 30–40 items including clothes, shoes, and outerwear — in a cohesive color palette. The idea is quality over quantity: every piece earns its place and pairs easily with the rest.</p>

<h2>The timeless essentials</h2>
<p>Start with these building blocks. Choose neutral colors (black, white, navy, beige, grey) so everything mixes effortlessly.</p>
<ul>
  <li><strong>A well-fitting white shirt.</strong> Dress it up under a blazer or down with jeans.</li>
  <li><strong>A crisp pair of jeans</strong> in a flattering, classic wash.</li>
  <li><strong>Tailored trousers</strong> in a neutral tone for work and evenings.</li>
  <li><strong>A little black dress</strong> that works for almost any occasion.</li>
  <li><strong>A quality knit or two</strong> — a crewneck and a cardigan cover most days.</li>
  <li><strong>A structured blazer</strong> to instantly elevate any outfit.</li>
  <li><strong>A versatile coat</strong> in a neutral that layers over everything.</li>
  <li><strong>White sneakers + one pair of ankle boots + a simple heel.</strong></li>
  <li><strong>A everyday bag</strong> in a neutral leather that goes with it all.</li>
</ul>

<h2>How to build yours</h2>
<ul>
  <li><strong>Pick a color palette.</strong> Two or three neutrals plus one accent color keeps everything interchangeable.</li>
  <li><strong>Audit what you own.</strong> Keep the pieces you actually wear and that fit well; set aside the rest.</li>
  <li><strong>Fill the gaps intentionally.</strong> Note which essentials you're missing and add them one quality piece at a time.</li>
  <li><strong>Prioritise fit and fabric.</strong> A simple piece that fits beautifully always looks more expensive than a trendy one that doesn't.</li>
</ul>

<h2>Why it works</h2>
<p>With a capsule wardrobe, mornings get easier, you shop more intentionally, and you always look put-together. Once your foundation is set, a few seasonal pieces are all you need to keep things fresh — without the overflowing closet.</p>
