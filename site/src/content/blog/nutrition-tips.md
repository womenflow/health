Good nutrition is the foundation of good health. Learn how simple dietary changes can make a big difference.

## The Basics

Eating a balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean proteins is essential for maintaining good health.

## Key Principles

- **Hydration**: Drink plenty of water throughout the day
- **Balance**: Include all food groups in your meals
- **Moderation**: Watch portion sizes
- **Timing**: Eat regular meals to maintain energy

## Getting Started

Start by making one small change at a time. Small changes add up to big results over time.
