<p>When it's hot, linen is your best friend. Breathable, lightweight, and effortlessly chic, it keeps you cool while looking relaxed and put-together — that "just back from somewhere sunny" ease. Here's how to style linen for summer, and how to wear it well.</p>

<h2>Why linen is the perfect summer fabric</h2>
<p>Linen is woven to breathe — it's highly breathable, wicks moisture, and dries quickly, which makes it ideal for hot, humid days. It also has that effortless, lived-in texture that looks great with almost no effort. The soft wrinkles are part of the charm, not a flaw.</p>

<h2>1. Linen trousers, two ways</h2>
<p>Wide-leg linen trousers are a summer staple. For day, pair them with a fitted tank or a cropped tee and flat sandals. For evening, tuck in a silky camisole, add a heeled sandal, and you've got a breezy, elegant look that still feels cool.</p>

<h2>2. The linen shirt as a hero piece</h2>
<p>A relaxed linen shirt is endlessly versatile. Wear it buttoned with shorts, open over a swimsuit as a beach cover-up, or knotted at the waist over a dress. In white or a soft neutral, it goes with everything.</p>

<h2>3. The easy linen set</h2>
<p>A matching linen set — shorts or trousers with a coordinating top — is the ultimate low-effort, high-impact summer outfit. It looks intentional and pulled-together, but it's really just two easy pieces. Add slides and sunglasses and you're set.</p>

<h2>Linen styling tips</h2>
<ul>
  <li><strong>Embrace the wrinkles.</strong> Linen is meant to look relaxed — a little crumple is the point, so don't stress about keeping it crisp.</li>
  <li><strong>Stick to natural tones.</strong> White, beige, sand, and soft pastels feel fresh and show off linen's texture beautifully.</li>
  <li><strong>Balance the volume.</strong> If your bottoms are loose and wide, keep your top fitted (and vice versa) for a clean shape.</li>
  <li><strong>Layer for the breeze.</strong> An open linen shirt over a tank adds an easy layer for cooler evenings without the heat.</li>
  <li><strong>Keep it simple.</strong> Linen looks best with minimal, natural accessories — a straw bag, gold jewelry, simple sandals.</li>
</ul>

<h2>The takeaway</h2>
<p>Linen is the easiest way to stay cool and look effortlessly stylish all summer. Choose natural tones, embrace the relaxed texture, and build simple outfits around a few breezy pieces.</p>
