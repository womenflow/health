Every woman deserves a wardrobe filled with pieces that never go out of style. These timeless classics form the foundation of any great wardrobe.

## The White Button-Up Shirt
The white button-up shirt is the ultimate versatile piece. Quality matters here; invest in a well-made cotton or silk blend.

## Classic Blue Jeans
A perfectly fitting pair of blue jeans is non-negotiable. Opt for a dark wash that won't fade quickly.

## Black Blazer
A well-tailored black blazer instantly elevates any outfit.

## Neutral Sweater
A quality sweater in cream, gray, or black is essential for layering.

## White Sneakers
Clean white sneakers bridge the gap between casual and put-together.

These classics will serve you for years to come!
