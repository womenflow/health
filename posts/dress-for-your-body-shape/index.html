How to Dress for Your Body Shape: A Simple Guide

2026-07-07

The secret to looking great in your clothes isn't a trend or a price tag — it's fit. Dressing for your body shape simply means choosing cuts that balance your proportions and highlight what you love. These are guidelines, not rules: wear what makes you feel confident. Here's a simple starting point.

First, find your general shape

Most people loosely fit one of these — and many are a mix. Don't overthink it; it's just a starting point.

  • Hourglass — shoulders and hips balanced, defined waist.
  • Pear (triangle) — hips wider than shoulders.
  • Apple (round) — weight carried around the middle, great legs.
  • Rectangle — shoulders, waist, and hips fairly even.
  • Inverted triangle — shoulders wider than hips.

Simple styling ideas by shape

  • Hourglass: Play up your waist. Wrap dresses, high-waisted bottoms, and fitted (not baggy) pieces show off your natural balance.
  • Pear: Draw the eye up. Detailed or brighter tops, off-shoulder necklines, and darker, straight or bootcut bottoms balance the hips.
  • Apple: Elongate and define. V-necks, empire waists, flowy tops over fitted bottoms, and showing off your legs work beautifully.
  • Rectangle: Create curves. Peplum tops, belted waists, ruffles, and layering add shape and definition.
  • Inverted triangle: Add volume below. A-line skirts, wide-leg trousers, and simpler necklines balance broader shoulders.

The rules that matter more than shape

  • Fit first. The right size that skims your body always looks better than "on-trend" that pulls or bags.
  • Tailoring is magic. A small nip at the waist or hem can transform an inexpensive piece.
  • Define a waist — a belt or a tucked-in top instantly makes most outfits look intentional.
  • Wear what you love. Confidence flatters every shape more than any styling tip.

The takeaway

Dressing for your shape is really about understanding your proportions and choosing pieces that make you feel great. Use these as a loose guide, then break every rule the moment something makes you feel amazing.
